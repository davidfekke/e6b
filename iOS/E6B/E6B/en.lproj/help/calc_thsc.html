True Heading/Speed for Course

Given the current wind direction and speed, and the true ground course and speed, calculate your aircraft's true heading and true air speed.

Sample Problem

Wind direction is 120° and speed is 15kt. Your true course is 200° and ground speed is 100 kt.

Answers:

Entering the data you discover:

True Heading 191.81
True Airspeed 103.66 kt
