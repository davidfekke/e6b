True Airspeed

This calculation will compute true airspeed and mach number given a pressure altitude, outside temperature and calibrated airspeed. This also calculates the density altitude.

Sample Problem

Pressure altitude is 10,000 ft, outside temperature is 2°C, and calibrated airspeed is 200 kt

Answers:

Entering the data you discover:

True Airspeed 234.49 kt
Mach Number 0.36
Density Altitude 10792.88 ft
