Managing Calculations

All of your weight and balance calculations are stored on your device's memory. You can add or remove new calculations or duplicate an existing calculation.

Add a new calculation

To add a new calculation press the '+' button in the lower left.

To delete existing calculations

Press the 'Edit' button in the upper left. Select the calculation you wish to delete by pressing the red delete button on the left, and verify that you wish to delete the calculation. In edit mode you can also reorder your weight and balance calculations by touching the grab handle on the right and dragging the calculation to it's new position.

To duplicate an existing calculation

While in 'Edit' mode, select the calculation you wish to duplicate, and press the "Duplicate" button in the lower left.
