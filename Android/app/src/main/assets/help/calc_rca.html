Required Calibrated Airspeed

This calculation will compute the calibrated airspeed to achieve a particular true airspeed given the pressure altitude and outside temperature. This also calculates the mach number and density altitude.

Sample Problem

Pressure altitude is 10,000 ft, outside temperature is 2°C, and true airspeed is 110 kt

Answers:

Entering the data you discover:

Calibrated Airspeed 93.46
Mach Number 0.17
Density Altitude 10792.88 ft
